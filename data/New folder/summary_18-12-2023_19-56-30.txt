Google Meet recordings are part of the enterprise version of G Suite and need to be enabled by your tech administrators. When in a Meet, click the 3-dot button to start recording. When you're done, click the 3-dot button again and select Stop Recording. The recording will be saved to your Google Drive and you can share the link with anyone, inside or outside your school, to view the video.
