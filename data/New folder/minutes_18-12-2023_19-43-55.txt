* • Introduction to recording a Google Meet Video Conference, and how it is a premium feature available free due to the COVID-19 situation
* • Ability to record is part of the enterprise version of G Suite, and needs to be turned on in the G suite Admin console
* • Start up a Google Meet as usual, and let other attendees know the meeting is being recorded
* • To start recording, click the three dots button, choose record meeting and accept to begin
* • Recordings saved to Google Drive and link available from Google Classroom
* • Share video from Google Drive with specific people or get shareable link
* • For anyone outside of the organization, change to “anyone with the link” in drop-down menu
* • Download video to computer via right-clicking on video or three dots button in Google Drive
