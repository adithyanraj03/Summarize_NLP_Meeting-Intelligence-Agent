As part of the premium G Suite, it may be added free due to the COVID-19 pandemic. It needs to be enabled by a technology administrator and the directions for recording are to start a Google Meet, let people know if they need to be informed, click the three dots button and choose record, click accept and the meeting is now recording. To end the recording click the three dots button and choose stop recording. The video will be stored in Google Drive and can be shared in many ways.
