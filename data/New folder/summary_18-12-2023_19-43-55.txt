Part of the enterprise version of G Suite, Google has enabled its premium feature free due to the COVID-19 situation. To record, start a Google Meet, click the three dots button in the bottom right corner and choose "Record Meeting". Invitees must be notified and Google admins can enable/disable recording in G Suite Admin console. To share, use Google Drive to share with specific people or Get a Shareable Link to allow anyone to watch. Download a copy by right-clicking on video.
